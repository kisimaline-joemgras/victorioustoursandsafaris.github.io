Three Days Safari

Arrival

On arrival at Kilimanjaro Airport or Arusha Airport. Transfer to lodge/Hotel for dinner and overnight (40 km, 30 minutes).

Day One

You will go to Mtowambu town, located at the bottom of the Great Rift Valley, after breakfast for a guided village tour. You will have the chance to observe normal Tanzanian daily life in this small but bustling town where residents from all 120 of the country's tribes coexist. Your local walking tour guide will take you to rice and banana plantations where you may engage with the farmers there. You will also visit a school, a market, and a carving factory where the renowned "Makonde" carvers various figures out of ebony black wood. After that, you'll proceed to the Lake Manyara National Park for a game drive and picnic lunch.You will get the chance to witness many monkeys, giraffes, elephants, impalas, dik-dik, flamingos in the lake, and if you're lucky, tree climbing lions in this small but stunning park with the lush forest. Later, you'll travel to the lodge to have dinner and spend the night.

Day Two

You can see many monkeys, giraffes, elephants, impalas, dik-dik, flamingos in the lake, and if you're lucky, tree climbing lions in this little, incredibly beautiful park with the lush forest. Later, you'll travel to the lodge for supper and an overnight stay.

Day Three

You will go for a full day of game viewing in Tarangire National Park after an early breakfast. Elephants are most frequently spotted in this park bathing in the Tarangire River. There are also thousands of giraffes, monkeys, impalas, and birds in this park. One of the best spots to watch lions, leopards, and other big cats is there as well. You return to Arusha in the late afternoon to prepare for your departure.
